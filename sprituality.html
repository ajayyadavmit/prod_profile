Kali Mata Yantra

Yantra is an instrument that helps to reach Sat-Chit-Anand (Truth-Consciousness/ Pure awareness- Happiness/ Joy). These capacities are stored inside human beings. However, they need to put an effort to find out Truth-Consciousness-Joy inside you. Kali Mata Yantra will guide you at the initial phase to find T-C-J. The outer four rectangle lines in a yantra represent the Fire (temperature), Earth(solid), Space (Area), and Water. By these four elements, the human physical body is constructed and after death the human body will be part of those four entities. The innermost dot at yantra is to find the pure awareness within yourself. Those between outer and inner elements are just a traversal element that help to reach at Conscious level. All things are accepted towards Kali. Kali is Universal so there is nothing any physical entity that is not accepted by Kali Mata. The only significance is you need to perform a task in a specific process for anything offered to Goddess.
